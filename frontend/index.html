We have hidden 4 devices somewhere in the Junction venue. The goal of the game is to try to find where they are located! All you need is to walk around with your phone or WiFi enabled device.

The circles represent each hidden station. Every second we will update the color of the station that shows how close you are to it. If the station is Black? that means it is not even picking you up. When you get extremely close by, you will get an alert! Just simply enter your MAC address to start getting updates.

To find your MAC address:

Android:
Go to Settings > About phone > Wi-Fi MAC address

iPhone: Go to Settings > General > About > Wi-Fi Address

Also we included a hint "Play Sound" button. If enough players click this button, we will play a brief tune on the associated device! You only get to press this once, use it wisely!
